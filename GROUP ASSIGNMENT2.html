BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

The department of entrepreneurship and business studies is one of the three departments that constitute the school of entrepreneurship and management technology (SEMT)in the federal university of technology, Minna. It was established in 2009/10 academic session as a reaction to the concern of the federal government and directive of the national universities commission (UNC) on the need to produce graduates with academic and professional skill the will promote self-employment in order to be employers of labour and reduce over dependence on paid employment Know more about us

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

The Department of Project Management of F.U.T Minna runs a comprehensive Project and logistics technology programme with emphasis on the development of the students' potential and skill in problem solving that is targeted to self employment. The programme has therefore been carefully drawn up to include all components of Maritime,Aviation,road,rail,and pipeline planning and management. The students are alsoexposed to a wide range of laboratory practical and workshop practice aimed at sharpening their practical skills neccesary to complement the analytical classroom lessons. In addition,the students gain further practical experience through the Students' Work Experience Programme Know more about us
