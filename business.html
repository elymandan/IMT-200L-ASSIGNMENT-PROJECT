The department of entrepreneurship and business studies is one of the three departments that constitute the school of entrepreneurship and management technology (SEMT)in the federal university of technology, Minna. It was established in 2009/10 academic session as a reaction to the concern of the federal government and directive of the national universities commission (UNC) on the need to produce graduates with academic and professional skill the will promote self-employment in order to be employers of labour and reduce over dependence on paid employment

The department has the vision to be a leading graduate academic department in entrepreneurship incubation and innovation through applied technology in the field of entrepreneurship and business studies. She also has the mission to develop high human capital through skill and dynamic training research and development service to meet the demands of the society for sustainable development. Hence our motto is “Empowerment for self-reliance” The department commenced with 28 students mostly drawn from remedial program of the university with seven (7) academic staff and four (4) nonacademic staff members. In 2011 the department passed the resources accreditation test from NUC and by2012 the department secured full accreditation status to ward B.Tech in the entrepreneurship and business studies (a double honours degree).the department has graduated three set of undergraduate students.

The department has made tremendous progress in diverse ways, for instance, the student populations is presently five Hundred and twenty (520) undergraduate student the department course offer student opportunities in specialization in marketing, entrepreneurship, management and finance. Further more the department offer post-graduate diploma in business management technology, master’s degree and Phd in entrepreneurship. The staff capacity of the department has really improved as two (2), lecturers on Phd study fellowship and three (3) others that went for master degree programs have resumed duty. Presently the department has one professor, one senior lecturer seven (7) lecturers lecturer I, two (2) lecturer II, four (4) Assistant lecturers and Five (5) graduate assistants the department of entrepreneurship and business studies is a unique and dynamic force that is committed to it mandate of driving technological advancement in this university and the country at large.

Suffice to say that, entrepreneurship is the process of creating values by bring together a unique of resources to exploit opportunities for social and commercial purpose it is not synonymous to being a business man, trader, self-employed, small scale business owners or vocational skills acquisition. Thus it is obvious that not all business people are entrepreneurs but the knowledge of business is essential for effective entrepreneurship. Most of our student, fresh from the secondary schools need basic skills to demonstrate the entrepreneurial behavior hence the introduction of vocational clubs to enhance skills acquisition. Lastly, the annual EBS week features diverse innovative programs to showcase the department and her relevance as well as collaborate with lovers of Nigeria to fulfill our mandate.

Long live FUTMinna long live SEMT, and Long live the EBS department
